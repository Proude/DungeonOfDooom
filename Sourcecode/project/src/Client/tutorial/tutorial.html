Instructions


The dungeon of doom is an online multiplayer game, which starts with registration of player. The game icludes the functionality to choose either single player or multi-player as well as an option to view top score achivied by player.

The objective of the game is to collect the specified amount of gold in the dungeon and get to the exit before other player.
